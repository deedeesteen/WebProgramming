COLLABORATIVE TELEPRESENCE

What do we mean by Collaborative Telepresence?


Let us examine the two words separately to get a better understanding...

  • According to Collins English Dictionary, "telepresence" refers to the use of virtual reality technology to operate machinery by remote control or to create the effect of being at a different or imaginary location.
  • "Collaborative" describes two or more people working together for a special purpose (Cambridge Dictionary, n.d.).

After reading these definitions, it is safe to say the Collaborative Telepresence refers to a group of individuals using virtual reality and video technology to come together to fulfill a purpose; for example, to have business meetings with persons from international corporations.
